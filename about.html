How 18Spar came to life

18Spar was born from a simple frustration: most small businesses couldn't afford great web design, and most agencies charging premium prices weren't delivering premium results.

Srinath, a self-taught full-stack developer from India, decided to change that. Starting with freelance projects in 2021, he built a reputation for delivering stunning, high-performance websites at accessible price points.

By 2023, 18Spar became a full agency ‚Äî a small but mighty team united by the belief that every business deserves a website that truly represents them.
