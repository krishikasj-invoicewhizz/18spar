FAQ

Common questions

Do you offer custom quotes?

Absolutely! Every business is unique. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote tailored to your specific needs and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, Razorpay, and PayPal. We typically take a 50% deposit upfront and the remainder upon project completion.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can start with a Starter plan and upgrade to Business or Premium as your needs grow. We'll apply a credit for your initial investment.

What's included in "free support"?

Free support covers bug fixes, minor content updates, and technical assistance during the included period. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages.

Do you provide hosting?

We can recommend and set up hosting for you, or deploy to your preferred hosting provider. Hosting costs are separate from project fees.
